Alaminos Pangasinan

Alaminos City, Pangasinan

Escape the hustle and bustle of Manila and discover the hidden gem of Alaminos, Pangasinan. Located just a few hours away from the capital, Alaminos offers a perfect retreat for travelers seeking a blend of nature, culture, and adventure.

Getting to Alaminos from Manila is convenient and hassle-free. Whether you prefer to drive or take public transportation, you'll find various options to suit your preferences. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the scenic journey as you traverse through picturesque landscapes and charming towns.

Once you arrive in Alaminos, prepare to be enchanted by its natural wonders and rich heritage. Here are some activities that await you:
Hundred Islands National Park: Embark on an island-hopping adventure and explore the breathtaking beauty of Hundred Islands. With over a hundred scattered limestone islets surrounded by crystal-clear waters, this national park offers endless opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and beach bumming. Don't miss iconic spots like Governor's Island, Quezon Island, and Children's Island.
